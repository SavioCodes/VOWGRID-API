# VowGrid Brand Guide

## Positioning

- Name: `VowGrid`
- Tagline: `Every AI action needs context, permission, and proof.`
- Product frame: the trust layer between AI agents and real-world actions

## Voice

- Precise
- Calm
- High-signal
- Security-grade
- Enterprise, not playful

## Visual direction

- Dark-first control-plane presentation
- Dense information with strong spacing discipline
- Blue as the primary action and focus color
- Mint for positive trust signals
- Amber for human attention and review
- Red reserved for failure and irreversible risk

## Core palette

- Graphite Black: `#0B1020`
- Steel Navy: `#121A2B`
- Electric Blue: `#4F7CFF`
- Mint Signal: `#2ED3B7`
- Warning Amber: `#F5B942`
- Soft White: `#F7F9FC`

## Typography

- Primary UI type: `Inter`
- Technical surfaces: `JetBrains Mono`
- Display style: tight tracking and high contrast, but never decorative for its own sake

## Brand rules

- Risk should be visible, not theatrical.
- Avoid neon, crypto aesthetics, and glass-heavy gimmicks.
- Show proof and rollback posture as first-class product concepts.
- Use gradients sparingly to create atmosphere, not noise.
